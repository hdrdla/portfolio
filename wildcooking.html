Proposal

Objective:

The objective is to build a functional, and clean website that highlights Wild Cooking's company mission and projects, and invites interested parties to contact them. The website will be:

  • • Built on Squarespace
  • • 3-pages (Home, 2 project pages)
  • • Responsive design based on the designs provided
  • • GDPR Compliant cookie banner

Design Fees & Terms:

  • • Fee includes website development and launch
  • • Fee includes 2 minor revisions of the final working design
  • • Additional revisions or requests made out of scope will be billed hourly at a rate of €30 per hour.
  • • Website hosting (Squarespace) and domain fees are not included

Terms in full will be outlined in the contract.

Quote: €700


Invoicing Terms:

  • • Deposit of 50% of total fee must be paid in full before any development services can begin
  • • Final 50% of total fee must be paid in full following the last of 2 revisions, and before website launch
  • • Invoices are subject to net 7 terms and interest of .05% will incur thereafter
  • • Fees may be paid via bank transfer, using Transferwise or credit card using Stripe with 1.6% surcharge.

Timeline: 2 weeks

  • • Design and content received
  • • Development of website
  • • First Revision
  • • Second Revision
  • • Testing and debugging
  • • Website launch

The project timeline is an estimate of the maximum amount of time that would be required to complete this project. The project length partly depends on receiving client feedback in a timely manner. Timeline begins once designs and content (copy and photos) are received.
